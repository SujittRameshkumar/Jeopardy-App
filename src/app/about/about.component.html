The Show

Jeopardy! is an American television game show created by Merv Griffin. The show features a quiz competition in which contestants are presented with general knowledge clues in the form of answers, and must phrase their responses in the form of questions. The current longest winning streak is held by the legend himself, Kenneth Wayne Jennings III.
